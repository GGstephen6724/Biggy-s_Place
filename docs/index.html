About Us

Welcome to Biggy's Place, a charming American restaurant located at 3701 Fowler St in Fort Myers, FL. This cozy spot offers a wide range of amenities, including takeout, delivery, vegetarian options, wheelchair accessibility, and even catering services. The restaurant also boasts a casual atmosphere, making it perfect for families, groups, and even kids. With features like private lot parking, free Wi-Fi, and gender-neutral restrooms, Biggy's Place ensures a comfortable dining experience for all guests.

Having visited Biggy's Place, I can attest to the positive feedback. The ambiance is welcoming, the service is attentive, and the food is simply delightful. From hearty breakfast dishes to satisfying lunch options, Biggy's Place certainly knows how to please its customers. Whether you're a local looking for a new favorite spot or a visitor seeking a taste of American comfort food, Biggy's Place is sure to leave you with a memorable dining experience.
